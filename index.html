About Manoj Kumar

Finance Made Simple,
Results Made Real

I'm Manoj Kumar, founder of Ashvamedha Finance, based in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. For over 6 years, I've worked one-on-one with Telugu-speaking families — helping them invest with clarity, pick the right insurance, and access pre-IPO opportunities most retail investors never see.

I started this because I saw too many people being sold wrong financial products, staying away from markets out of fear, or simply not knowing where to begin. My mission is simple: be the financial advisor I wish existed when I started — honest, clear, and in Telugu.

Whether it's your first SIP, your family's health cover, or investing in an unlisted company before it hits the stock market — I guide you through it personally, step by step.
